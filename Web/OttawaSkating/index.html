    What Is This?

    As a lover of hockey and ice skating, I am constantly going to the City of Ottawa's online Public Skating Schedule. While the City's site is a great resource, it's not exactly the most user friendly user interface. Results are paginated, restricting results is awkward and one cannot search for multiple sessions at the same time such as the elusive Public + Family Skate search.

    OttawaSkating.com is my attempt to simplify the search for skating times in Ottawa. The site makes it easy to filter down to exactly what you're looking for and get your results quickly. Scheduling data is taken directly from the City of Ottawaâ€™s Public Skating Schedule and processed for use in the site.

    And yes, posted cancellations ARE removed from the underlying data.

    Search by multiple session types and/or multiple rinks and have the data presented on a map as well as in tabular format. Scroll through each day while maintaining your search parameters.

    Everything is pretty straightforward and easy to use. The few nuances that exist are highlighted in the FAQ.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    There are three different ways of viewing the skating schedule.


    Use filters to limit the data to just the day, session type and locale you are looking for. Fine tune the data to exclude unwanted detail. (For example, after selecting "Skating" for Session Group, you can exclude Adult Skating when looking to take your kids.)


    Depending on the level of your zoom, some text may overlap. This is unavoidable when showing a lot of data on a map. Filter down to just the data you want to minimize text overlap. You can also zoom in and resize the map to your specific area which will provide more space for skating info for each rink to appear.
