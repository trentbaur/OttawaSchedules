Frequently Asked Questions

There are three different ways of viewing the swimming schedule.


Use filters to limit the data to just the day, session type and locale you are looking for. Fine tune the data to exclude unwanted detail. For example, after selecting "General" for Session Group, you can choose to exclude Wave.

The more you filter, the better your experience will be.


Depending on the level of your zoom, some text may overlap. This is unavoidable when showing a lot of data on a map. To minimize text overlap, filter down to just the data you want. You can also zoom in and resize the map to your specific area which will provide more space for swimming info for each pool to appear.


A couple known issues exist. First off, the map labels can show sessions out of chronological order. I am looking into a way of resolving this.


Another known issue is the By Time table seems to squeeze the text onto itself when there is a lot of data. The less data there is, the less of a problem this is. However, it still seems to remain. The odd thing is that the OttawaSkating.com site does not have a similar problem. I will continue to look into this.
