    What Is This?

    This site was born from my desire to create an easier way to find Skating sessions in Ottawa. The City's site is a great resource but sometimes it's can be cumbersome to survey all of the data. Results are paginated, restricting results is awkward and one cannot search for multiple sessions at the same time such as the elusive Public + Family Skate search.

    After creating OttawaSkating.com I decided to do the same with Ottawa's swimming schedule.

    OttawaSwimming.com simplifies the search for swim times in Ottawa by presenting the swimming sessions in multiple formats: By Arena, By Time and By Location on a map. Set the filters to exactly the session and location you want and the results immediately display. The data is also designed to make it easy to browse through schedules on a day to day basis after setting up session and location preferences.
